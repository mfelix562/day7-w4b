Coxinha

A popular Brazilian snack with a crunchy outside, and soft on the inside. You can have shredded chicken as your base protein, or a meat of your choice. Surrounding your protein is some great herbs and spices and a layer of dough securing your fresh snack.

Pastel

This appetizer has a thin crunchy layer surrounding the fillings of your choice! Some typical options can be cheese, protein with a cheese of your choice, or even guayaba (guava)!

Empanada

This delicious dish has a homemade layer of well baked dough surrounding the ingredients of your choice! The picture is an example of shredded chicken mixed with green onions, cilantro, chopped tomatoes, corn, and other spices and herbs to make the taste exquisite!
