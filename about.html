About Us

We wanted to start a company that is dedicated to making the most authentic brazilian dishes possible. There are a lot of people who travel to the US from Brazil, and it can be hard to adapt to a whole new country. Although it's much easier now and days to communicate with the internet, it's still hard to find the foods that taste like it does back home. This is where we decided to step in and take action!
Our chefs get their ingredients fresh every day from the smaller markets, and they're able to acquire the ingredients that would normally be hard to find here in the US. Everything that you see is made from scratch, and you can tell from the first bite to the last. Try it for yourself, and see what we're talking about!
